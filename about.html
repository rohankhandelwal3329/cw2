About Me

Hello! I'm Rohan Khandelwal, a passionate Computer Science student currently pursuing my degree at Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia. My journey into the world of technology began with a simple curiosity about how websites work, and it has since evolved into a deep passion for creating beautiful, functional, and user-friendly applications.

Throughout my academic career, I've developed a strong foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development. I specialize in Python and modern JavaScript frameworks, with hands-on experience working on both frontend and backend technologies. My coursework at GSU has included Data Structures & Algorithms, Web Development, Database Systems, and Software Engineering—all of which have prepared me for real-world challenges.

Beyond academics, I'm currently working as a Developer Intern at CETLOE, where I build real-world solutions while constantly learning and growing. I also run a YouTube channel with over 15,000 subscribers, maintaining a 400+ day streak of uploading daily videos. This experience has taught me the value of consistency, creativity, and connecting with an audience.

When I'm not coding or creating content, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, traveling to new destinations (I've visited 7 countries and 19 US states!), or hitting the gym to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
